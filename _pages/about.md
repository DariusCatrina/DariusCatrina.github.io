Hi, my name is Darius Catrina, originally from Romania but based in NYC for the moment. Currently I'm working at Google NYC (Youtube) ~ since May 2026 and doing research in Rohit Singh's lab @ Duke University where I'm focusing on protein generation. 

Currenty, I'm very interesting in applying AI to better understand the world of proteins -- having worked on projects ranging from protein embedders & plms to 3d structure predicition and 3D sequence to 3D sequence translation. Soon I might leave industry for a PhD but who knows!

Previously, I worked under Bhuwan Dhingra in the DukeNLP lab. Also I have interned at Apple & Google. Most of this work was around representation theory, more precisely creating descriptive embeddings for text & sound.
